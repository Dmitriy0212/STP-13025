How to play

  • Match identical objects and clear
    every board
  • Search Through Puzzle
    Collections
  • Find hidden matching items
    inside crowded 3D spaces.
  • Create Triple Matches
  • Collect three identical objects to
    remove them instantly.
  • Complete Puzzle Levels
  • Clear every object to progress
    through gameplay.
  • Use Helpful Boosters
  • Hints and tools assist during difficult
    stages.
  • Unlock New Challenges
  • Progress through increasingly detailed
    puzzle environments.
