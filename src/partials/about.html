About the game

A relaxing puzzle experience powered by colorful 3D matching Take the matching games you already know and love, but now everything's in 3D and way more cluttered - in a good way. The game focuses on smooth progression, satisfying visual feedback, and casual mobile accessibility while introducing increasingly creative puzzle layouts and object themes.  We want it to look sleek, help you unwind, and give you that little dopamine hit when you clear a level.
